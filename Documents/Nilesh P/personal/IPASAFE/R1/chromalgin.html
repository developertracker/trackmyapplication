Chromalgin®

Alginate impression materials have been a staple of dental practice. However, their hygroscopic nature and their tendency to deform in humid weather make storage and handling a bit worrisome. Dusting of dental alginates is another problem and dust free alginate comes at a premium. We believe that dental alginates being dust free is hygiene to the product. Our dust free dental alginate - Chromalgin® is available in a convenient pack of 24 single dose sachets which make handling problems a thing of the past. We also offer the 454 gm pack for those who need the bigger pack.

  • Dust free

  • 2 color changes to indicate mixing and setting stages

  • Fresh new flavours based on dentists' suggestions - Chocolate, Strawberry

  • Two pack sizes - Single use sachets & the usual 454 gm

  • Easy mix

Two color changes to indicate mixing and setting stages

Chromalgin® is a two-step color changing powder. It changes color during mixing and acquires another color once the impression is set. Taking feedback from clinicians, we made Chromalgin® in refreshingly new strawberry and chocolate flavours, both favourites with children as well as adults.

  • Light PINK in the mixing time

  • DARK PINK in the working time

  • CARAMEL in the mouth

  • Light PINK in the mixing time

  • DARK PINK in the working time

  • PINK in the mouth
