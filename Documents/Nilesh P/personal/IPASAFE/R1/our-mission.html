Our Mission

We are on our way of becoming an idea magnet for path-breaking R&D in medical sciences by creating a platform for reaching out to the entire country, and even the world, with innovative products.

Chronic and stress related diseases such as diabetes are on the rise in India. So have the frequency of dental ailments. With over 80% of population having some dental ailments or the other, India already has the world's largest patient pool for dentistry. However, dentistry in India remains most underserviced.*
*National Oral Health Survey and fluoride mapping 2002-2003

Piscium® was born with a mission to channel the latest technologies
for solving common dental problems through leveraging breakthrough innovations. Our team understands research as well as innovation commercialization. There is immense R&D knowledge that resides in
this country awaiting a launch pad.

Piscium® will be this launch pad and in turn create value for both - the scientists and its shareholders. In this endeavour, we pledge that quality shall be the first aspect in all stages of innovation and development.
