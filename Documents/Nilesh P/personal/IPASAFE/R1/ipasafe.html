Alcohol-based
Hand Sanitizer

Frequent disinfection of hands using sanitizers have become an absolute necessity. Alcohol-based hand sanitizers are usually the preferred mean for routine hand hygiene as they are faster, more effective, and better tolerated by hands than washing with soap and water. Hand sanitizers may be particularly useful for preventing the spread of current pandemic caused by SARS-CoV-2.

Piscium’s IPSAFE sanitizer is an alcohol-based hand sanitizer formulated for use in hospitals and healthcare facilities, homes, offices and congregation points for effective disinfectant action.
